How to reduce and manage
negative thoughts

  • To change anything you need to develop awareness, you need to be aware when you are experiencing negative thoughts, to notice when you feel low in confidence or mood such as, nervous, uneasy or ‘down’. Pay attention to the thoughts and /or images you are having at that time. It is these thought patterns that are negatively influencing your confidence and mood.
  • Whenever you detect that you are feeling uncomfortable or experiencing unpleasant and negative thoughts you simply call “STOP!” (In your head or out loud if that is suitable).
  • Notice how this effectively halts the machinery of negative thinking, causing it momentarily to cease.
  • Negative thoughts literally STOP for a moment, creating a window of opportunity to direct your thoughts elsewhere. Take the opportunity to redirect your thoughts as described in the following technique.
  • To practise the technique you can leave a short interval after the STOP and then deliberately restart the process of having your worrying thoughts (they may happen automatically anyway if you didn’t redirect your thoughts), and then at irregular intervals you call “STOP!”
  • You may find that you have negative thought patterns that appear as visual images rather than internal dialogue. So be sensitive and notice the specific worry images that you have when you feel ill at ease. The STOP technique works just as well with images as it does with words.
  • Research evidence suggests that to change a behaviour it needs to be practised for a minimum of 21 days.

    • To help you to be aware of your ‘worry’ thoughts and images, the ones that will trigger the STOP, identify an experience when you had a strong negative emotion or severe loss of confidence. Reconnect with that experience now as realistically as possible. Allow and notice the thoughts and images that come to you. These will be some of your frequent negative thoughts and /or images that you can choose to stop. Record them here. Practice the thought stopping technique.
